Levels.

Levels are the most important tool that is in any financial markets, including cryptors. Levels are those places where there is an imbalance, between buyers and sellers. When you see, a repeated rebound of the price from the level, this is essentially the level on which the imbalance occurred. A reasonable question may arise; "Why does the levels attract the attention of traders so much?" It's very simple, in these levels there is a cluster of limit orders, and in both sides. Because, everything obviously where to set the stop. Not only robots are traded on the market, but also the real people who enter the market, not because he wanted, but according to his trading system. The feet, as a rule, arrange a little higher / lower levels. Levels will help improve your trading, regardless of the strategy on which you are trading.

EUR/USD

Today we will look at several different levels. We will learn about such levels as strong, weak and open.

When the market goes down or up, the price reaches a certain level, and the more the number of bounces from this level, the stronger the level becomes. Thus, we can start purchases from a strong level, in the hope of rebound. We define the strong levels, only by candle bodies.

It is worth noting that the level cannot be called a straight line that will definitely work. Levels are better noted in the form of the price range in which the price may come. You should not wait for the price very accurately, it may not reach your level, and turn out. I also note that strong levels are at high timeframes, and they are always confirmed by volumes. I think you understand that in turn, weak levels are in small timeframes.

It also happens such things as a false breakdown - this is when the price went lower / above the level, but immediately returned, to the level. In this case, the level is not considered punched.

Open levels are levels that are not blocked in the future. You need to build open levels by shadows, the larger the shadow, the stronger this open level. The main function of open levels is the fact that the price is returned to them. As a rule, open levels are extremes that are made by large players. That is, at such levels, there are huge volumes, and the price returns back. At such levels, you need to put limit order orders, in the hope that the price will reach the level and turns sharply.

Levels in trade will be guaranteed useful when working with any strategy. Therefore, before entering the market, do not be lazy to mark them on the chart. They will indicate you where it is better to enter the market, and help you set stop loss.
